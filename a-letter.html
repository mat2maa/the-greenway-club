A Letter From The Previous Owners

To: Falls River Swim Club members and friends

It is with great pleasure we announce that Falls River Swim Club LLC is now under new ownership by The Greenway Club at Falls River Inc. The Greenway Club is owned by Braxton Wilson, Chris Carver and Ben Everhart, who also own Aquatic Management Group, the pool management company that has provided pool and lifeguard management services for your Club for the last three years. They are starting on day one with a solid foundation of years of management experience at your Club.

The Club was not being actively marketed for sale when they approached us recently expressing an interest. We value and respect the ambition. As some of you may know, our family moved back to our home town a couple of years ago. We decided it would be in the best interest of our family, the Club and members to restore a renewed and reenergized enthusiasm together with local ownership back to your Club.

We greatly appreciate and want say thanks for the tremendous feeling of family and friendship we have received from so many of you over the last 8 years of ownership. We pass on the Club with confidence that as you get to know Braxton, Chris and Ben as we have, that the warm sense of family and community at the Club will continue for years to come. Without hesitation we can share with you that in our career lifetime we would rank Braxton, Chris and Ben up in the top few percent of workers in terms of their work ethic, ingenuity, customer focus and commitment to quality. We want you to know publicly what we told them privately: We can't wait to see how they develop the Club! We simply won't be surprised to hear in the years ahead that your Club has become one of the hardest Club's in the region in which to get a membership. We deeply wish to hear of their success as well as your continued enjoymnent in the years to come.

It has been a pleasure serving you these last 8 years. We are proud of what we have accomplished at the pool during our time: reinvesting over $100,000 back into your Club while keeping long term member dues rates lower today than a decade ago. We have reinvested to replace or upgrade every one of the filtration pumps, slide pump and motors (they circulate about 4 billion pounds of water a year); the slide pool has been freshly replastered; over 20,000 square feet of pool deck have been resurfaced; most all of the pool furniture has been purchased new or reconditioned to like new. And we are proud to leave behind one of the only pools in the region to have live bands every Friday in the summer, a free-to-use private poolside party area, and a swim team with 6 Division Championships in the last decade. We wish you all the best in the future.

Sincerely

Brett & Karen McAvoy
