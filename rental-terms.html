Rental Terms


WHO MAY RESERVE THE CLUBHOUSE FOR A PRIVATE EVENT?

You do not need to be a Club member to rent the facility for a private event. Rentals are available from late September through April.
We do not rent the clubhouse during the pool season.


HOW DO I RESERVE THE FACILITY?

Please contact the Club manager by phone or email to check availability first. Then complete the Rental Agreement and Credit Card Authorization Form. If you are a Club member, you will not need to complete the Credit Card Authorization Form. Submission of the application and payment to the Club manager secures the rental.


SECURITY DEPOSIT

A security deposit is due at the time the contract is signed before the contract is final. If any terms of the contract are broken, some or all of the security deposit may be forfeited and responsibility is not limited to the amount of the security deposit if the Club incurs damages in excess of the security deposit as a result of the rental.


ALCOHOL

Contact Alex at Café Buongiornos at 919-896-7459 for all your catering and bar needs.


CLEAN UP

Cleanup procedure includes vacuuming the carpet, returning furniture to the original position, wiping and cleaning surfaces, as well as any other work required to return the facility to the same condition as it was upon arrival. Trash should be taken to the dumpster, located at the corner of the parking lot, near the soccer field.

Unless a clean up fee is paid, the facility must be cleaned within the time of this rental contract. If clean-up services are purchased on the date of the rental event or if the facility is not fully cleaned prior to the end of the rental, a clean-up fee equal to 1½ times the clean-up fee rate will be deducted from the security deposit..


TIME

If extra time is needed to decorate, that time must be included in the rental agreement. Any contracted work (i.e. caterers or DJ's) must also comply with the agreed upon time. All events must end by the contract end time.


NOISE

In compliance with the Wake County noise ordinance, all rentals are required to play any music in moderation, so that it does not disturb area residents. If noise complaints are reported, Greenway Swim Club reserves the right to end the rental early without refund of rental payment and security deposit. The noise ordinance is strictly enforced after 11:00pm. It is the renter's responsibility to make any contracted workers and guests aware of this ordinance.


RIGHT TO ACCESS

GSC management reserves the right to access the Clubhouse during the rental period.


LIMITATIONS OF USE

GUESTS ARE BY INVITATION ONLY. The Clubhouse can not be used for events that are open to the public. This includes for-profit and not-for-profit events.

APPLICANT MUST BE 25 AND UP AT THE EVENT. The applicant signing this rental agreement must be 25 years of age or older and present for the entire duration of the rental period.

FIREPLACE USE. Regular wood may not be burned in the fireplace. Only manufactured logs may be used. All fires must be out completely before the end of the rental time. Use of wood in fireplace will result in loss of security deposit.

ADULT PRESENCE. Events with attendees under the age of 21 need to have at least 1 adult over the age of 30 for every 10 people under the age of 21 at the Clubhouse during the entire duration of the event.

NO SMOKING. Greenway Swim Club grounds are smoke free. Smoking is not permitted inside or outside the Clubhouse. Smoking may result in damages that affect the return of the security deposit.

NO GLASS OUTSIDE CLUBHOUSE. This is a pool facility where people walk barefoot outside. To protect the safety of the membership, glass is not permitted outside the Clubhouse. If during the rental any glass gets onto the deck or into the pool, the renter agrees to pay for all costs associated with draining, cleaning, and refilling the pool as required by Wake County law.

NO ANIMALS. Wake County Health codes prohibit animals inside the Clubhouse.

LOCK UP. It is the renter's responsibility to make sure that the facility is locked upon leaving. This includes all windows and doors.

GUEST SAFETY. The renter agrees to limit the activity of all people present to those activities that do not put personal safety or the safety of others at risk.

DECORATIONS or facility alterations may not be used that will cause damage to the walls, wood, floor, windows, or any other part of Club property, or that result in permanent changes to the facility. Please inform all people setting up for the event.

Items not intended to be thrown out must be removed during rental period. Anything brought to the facility must be brought in and removed from Club property during the rental contract period.

AREA AVAILABLE FOR USE. This rental agreement limits use of the facility to the area inside the Clubhouse as well as the deck space that is not inside the fenced area of the pool. Any additional space required for the event must be pre-approved.

HOME THEATER SYSTEM. Configured to play up to 5 DVD's and CD's.


PERSONAL PROPERTY

Renter assumes financial responsibility for all items brought onto Club property. Greenway Swim Club is not responsible for the loss or damage to any items not owned by Greenway Swim Club.


REFUND POLICY

100% refund if canceled in writing 60 days in advance.

50% refund if canceled in writing less than 60 days and more than 30 days in advance.

50% credit toward future Clubhouse rental if canceled in writing less than 30 days and more than 7 days in advance.

No refunds or credits for cancellations less than 7 days in advance.
