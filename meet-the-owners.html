Dear Valued Members,

The Greenway Club at Falls River is proud to announce the purchase of Falls River Swim Club. The Greenway Club is fully owned and operated by Benjamin A. Everhart, Braxton P.Wilson, and Christopher E. Carver. Over the past 3 years we have provided turnkey management at the club in regards to staffing, maintenance, and repair as owners of Aquatic Management Group Inc.

We promise to be as transparent as possible with the membership and our vision for the club. We truly value your experience and want to ensure that we will surpass the high standards the club has developed over the years. Our vision of what we want to add to the club and our passion to maintain the tradition that makes the club unique are our top priorities.

As the new owners of the club, we look forward to meeting each of you on the pool deck. We are excited to be taking the helm of an exclusive club that we have come to know so well over the past few years. At any point we appreciate your feedback and are confident our passion fro safety, family, and fun will provide an enjoyable experience at The Greenway Club.
