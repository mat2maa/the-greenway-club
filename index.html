Welcome to The Greenway Club at falls river


Meet The Owners

Tucked away off the Capital Area Greenway Trail, the Greenway Club at Falls River offers a private, family driven experience, Where the focus is on safety and fun!

Don't miss out on our champion swim team, The Racers, which brings children of all ages together for wholesome and healthy activities.

Memberships Offer:
  • competition size swimming pool
  • beach entry slide pool
  • clubhouse
  • competition tennis courts
  • a competition volleyball court
  • billiards
  • covered & uncovered picnic and BBQ areas throughout
  • lovely lawn area where many family - friendly events are hosted throughout the summer.
  • Fully staffed snack bar, which is ready to provide cold drinks and quick meals/snacks.
