Rental FAQ


CAN I USE THE POOL DECK AND THE CLUBHOUSE DURING THE POOL SEASON?

You must be a Club member to rent the pools for after-hours private events. The clubhouse may be included. Please contact the Club manager for details.


CAN I HAVE A PARTY AT THE POOL DURING REGULAR POOL HOURS?

You may have a private party in our "Park at the Pool Private Party Area." Only Club members may reserve this space. The space is free of charge and may be reserved by the check-in attendant during pool hours or by the manager via phone call or email. Normal guest fees apply. There is a limit of 20 non-member guests for this space. There is no limit to the number of member guests.

Guests include anyone passing through check-in, regardless of length of stay or intent to swim.


HOW DO I RESERVE THE SPACE?

At your request, we can typically provide a 7 day hold on a date, but the clubhouse is not considered booked until the rental agreement has been accepted and payment has been made.


WHAT IS THE SQUARE FOOTAGE OF THE SPACE?

About 1200 sqft inside and another 1200 sqft on the Clubhouse deck - mostly covered under roof.


HOW MANY PEOPLE CAN THE SPACE ACCOMMODATE?

Permitted up to 99 people - the porch area is included, weather permitting. The Club has had inside-only parties up to about 80 people. Since space requirements are subjective, if in question, we recommend you come to see the Clubhouse.


HOW MANY TABLES/CHAIRS ARE THERE?
CAN I BRING IN MORE IF NECESSARY?

There are two bar height tables and a small bar area, with 14 bar height chairs. There are three square tables, with 12 chairs. There are four cushioned chairs and a sofa. There are also three 6ft folding tables available. On the porch, there are three wrought iron tables and 12 chairs. You may bring in additional tables and chairs if needed. For large sit down events some parties have rented tables and chairs from local party rental businesses.


CAN I USE A DJ?

Yes, there is space for a DJ or a small band.


CAN I SHOW A BIRTHDAY VIDEO?

Yes, we have equipment to allow you to show a DVD onto both TVs at once, allowing it to be seen from the entire space. You may also bring in projection equipment to show a video on the wall.


CAN I DECORATE?

Yes, but we ask that you not use nails or staples anywhere.


IS THE CLUBHOUSE DECORATED FOR CHRISTMAS?

Yes, we have a Christmas tree and several other tasteful decorations.


IS THERE A SINK/REFRIGERATOR?

The bar area has a small sink, an ice storage bin and a refrigeration unit that is 16"x65". Please ask that the refrigerator (cooler) be turned on if you plan touse it.


IS THERE A KITCHEN?

The kitchen space is not available for private events, unless catered by Cafe Buongiorno's.


CAN I USE THE POOL TABLE?

Yes, the equipment is kept on the table, under the cover.


WHAT IF I DON'T WANT THE POOL TABLE?

Just leave the cover on. The table is great for decorations, gifts or coats. Please do not use it for food or beverages. Please do not move the table.


CAN I USE THE POOL DECK FOR SPACE DURING
THE OFF-SEASON? WE WON'T BE SWIMMING.

No. The pool decks are locked and may not be used, due to city regulations.


CAN I BURN MY OWN LOGS?

Yes. But please - no wood. Wood creates smoke that takes days to dissipate, creating problems for the next renters. Out of respect for parties who follow, the Club requires the use of manufactured logs.


CAN I COME EARLY TO SET UP?

Out of respect for renters that come before the party, rental times are limited to the time block purchased. People with nighttime parties who need significant set up time should rent the afternoon rental block.


DO YOU HAVE WIFI?

Yes. Wireless internet access is available.


CAN I PLAY MY MP3, IPOD, DVDs ETC?

Yes. An entertainment system is available. We recommend testing your equipment and media prior to the event.
