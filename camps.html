CAMP STRUCTURE

Each week will have a different theme, with a craft activity revolving around that theme. Camps will utilize the two swimming pools, volleyball court, game room, soccer field, local parks, greenway trail and craft instructions. Camp staff is trained in American Red Cross CPR and First Aid. Counselor ratio to campers is 1:9.

MISSION STATEMENT

TO STRENGTHEN THE COMMUNITY THROUGH YOUTH DEVELOPMENT.

Each day enthusiastic Greenway Club counselors create a learning environment for your child to excel. Where the focus is on youth development each camper has the opportunity to learn new skills, gain new friends, and have FUN!

Each camper takes home an achievement report at the end of each week. For example, during field time campers will not only play games such as soccer, kickball and touch football, but campers will also learn about the games.

Being a team player and learning how to participate is at the core of each day at The Greenway Club.

Listed are learning activities each camper will have the option to participate in during camp.


SWIM AND SPORTS FOCUS

  • Adaptive Swim Programs (During the summer season)
  • Interactive athletic programs- soccer, tennis, volleyball, kickball etc
  • Healthy diets and living

ART AND CRAFTS FOCUS

  • Arts and Crafts
  • Science exploration
  • Designing and constructing
  • Bake/lemonade sells at the pool

TEAM BUILDING FOCUS

  • Outdoor and indoor activities
  • Communication
  • Diversity
